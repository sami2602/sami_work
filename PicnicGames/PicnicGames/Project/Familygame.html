  • 1: Croquet

    Kickstart your picnic with this riveting game, and you’re bound to have so much fun. A game of gentle precision and strategy, croquet requires a clear flat surface and a decent croquet set. Played in two teams, the game continues until one team navigates all their balls through the course first. This classic game taps into friendly competition and is suitable for older kids and adults alike.

    How To Play
    • Players play 9-wicket croquet using nine iron hoops (wickets)..
    • The objective of nine-wicket croquet is to be the first player to score 14 wickets and 2 stakes in the correct order
    • Players earn a wicket only if they strike the ball through the wicket in the correct direction
    • If you’re playing with boundaries around the court, and a ball goes out of play, there are rules to consider.

  • 2: Badminton

    A fun game that doesn’t require much setup, badminton is a hit at any outdoor gathering. All you need is a net, shuttlecocks, and rackets. The first team to score a predetermined number of points wins, making it a fast-paced, fun outdoor game for all the family.

    How To Play
    • Divide the players into teams of two.
    • Understand the object of the game
    • Get familiar with the badminton court.
    • Learn the basic ways to strike the shuttlecock.
    • Exploit your opponent's weaknesses

  • 3: Water Balloon Toss

    A fun game perfect for a hot day, water balloon toss is a sure way to provide plenty of laughs. During this game, two team members try to toss and catch a water balloon without popping it, and everybody enjoys the extra fun of a potential splash.

    How To Play
    • >Divide all of the participants into two-player teams. Each team is then given a water balloon.
    • When you say go, the player who is currently holding the water balloon must attempt to throw it to their partner.

  • 4. I Spy

    Games like hide and seek can be enjoyed by kids of all ages and grown-ups too. The best part of this picnic game is that you need no supplies.

    How To Play
    • Choose one player to be the spy, who has to count till 10 while keeping the eyes closed.
    • Every other player has to find a spot to hide before the spy finishes counting.
    • Spy has to scout the entire location and find each hiding player.

  • 5. Human Knot

    This exciting and challenging game requires acute thinking abilities to succeed.

    How To Play
    • Make all the players stand in a circle, facing inwards.
    • Everyone has to stretch their right arm out and hold the left hand of someone else. Ensure nobody grabs the hand of a person who is standing right next to them.
    • The fun starts now. All the kids have to work towards untangling the knot without letting the hands loose. The final goal is to form a full circle again.
    • Kids can go over the arms or under, pass through the legs or do whatever, but ensure the human knot does not get broken.

  • 6: Hit The Water Balloons

    Playing with water and balloons is a favorite among kids. To play this game, the only supplies you will need are water, a stone, and some balloons. This can be played individually or in teams.

    How To Play
    • Fill the balloons with water.
    • Place them in a row.
    • Make the child stand at a distance of at least 5 feet from the balloons.
    • However, make sure there are no kids standing in the line of the stone-throw.
